About Captibiz

We're a team of experienced business consultants dedicated to helping entrepreneurs turn their visions into successful, fundable businesses. With over 15 years of combined experience, we've helped hundreds of businesses secure millions in funding.

Our Story

Founded by seasoned business professionals with a passion for entrepreneurship and a commitment to making professional business consulting accessible to everyone.

Captibiz was born from a simple observation: too many great business ideas never see the light of day because entrepreneurs lack access to professional business planning services. Traditional consulting firms charge premium rates that put quality business plans out of reach for most startups and small businesses.

Our founders, with backgrounds in investment banking, management consulting, and entrepreneurship, decided to change that. By leveraging technology and streamlined processes, we've created a model that delivers the same quality as top-tier consulting firms at a fraction of the cost.

Since our founding, we've helped over 500 businesses across diverse industries secure more than $50 million in funding. Our success stories range from tech startups raising Series A rounds to immigrant entrepreneurs obtaining investor visas to established businesses securing SBA loans for expansion.
