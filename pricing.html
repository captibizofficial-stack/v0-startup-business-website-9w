Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our pricing and services.

What's included in the base price?

Each package includes everything listed in the features, with no hidden fees. You'll receive your completed business plan, all supporting documents, and the specified number of revision rounds.

How do you compare to competitors?

Our prices are 30-40% below traditional consulting firms while maintaining the same quality standards. We achieve this through efficient processes and technology integration.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment options including 50% upfront and 50% upon completion for packages over $500. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

What if I need revisions?

All packages include revision rounds as specified. Additional revisions beyond the included amount are available at $75 per round.

How long does the process take?

Delivery times are clearly specified for each package. Rush delivery options are available for an additional fee if you need your documents sooner.

Do you guarantee results?

While we can't guarantee loan approval or funding success, we do guarantee that your documents will meet professional standards and industry requirements. We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee.
